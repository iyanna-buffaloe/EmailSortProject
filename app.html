Why This Project Exists

Managing a high-volume inbox means making a lot of small decisions quickly ‚Äî what needs attention now, what can wait, and what isn't worth reading at all. Done manually, that process is tedious and something important can always slip through the cracks.

This tool automates that triage. It reads incoming emails, classifies them by type, and routes each one directly into the correct Trello list: Finance, Travel, Calendar, and more ‚Äî so the right messages surface immediately without digging through the noise. Anything urgent gets flagged before it gets lost.

Use the demo above to test it with any email and see how it gets categorized and turned into a Trello card in real time.
